Cereal Recipe

Description

A cereal is any grass cultivated for the edible components of its grain (botanically, a type of fruit called a caryopsis), composed of the endosperm, germ, and bran

Cereal grain crops are grown in greater quantities and provide more food energy worldwide than any other type of crop[1] and are therefore staple crops.

Ingredients

  • cereal of your choice
  • a bottle of water
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Pour the cereal in a bowl.
  2. Add the cup of milk.
  3. Pour in the water and mix, you're good to go.
