Cake Recipe

Description

Cake is a form of sweet food made from flour, sugar, and other ingredients, that is usually baked.

In their oldest forms, cakes were modifications of bread, but cakes now cover a wide range of preparations that can be simple or elaborate, and that share features with desserts such as pastries, meringues, custards, and pies.

Ingredients

  • 3 cup all purpose flour
  • 4 egg
  • 2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 1 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup butter

Steps

  1. Cream together butter and sugar.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, vanilla essence, milk and beaten eggs.
  3. Bake the cake as per your convenience.
  4. Check with a knife or skewer if it is cooked and serve.
